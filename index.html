Our story

Hi, I'm Pravallika

I'm the heart behind this small business journey, which started back in 2022. It all began during the COVID days when I stumbled across inspiring small business videos on my For You page. I couldn't stop thinking about starting something of my own, and with a little encouragement from my mom, I did just that!

Each year since then, I've explored something new: resin keychains in my first year, polymer clay earrings the next, then candles with soy wax, and last summer I brought back resin keychains and added coasters and bookmarks to the mix. It's been such a fun, creative ride, thanks to your amazing support, it keeps growing!

This year, I'm bringing back your favorite candles. Can't wait for you to see what I've been working on!
